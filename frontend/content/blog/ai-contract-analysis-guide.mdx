## What is AI Contract Analysis?

AI contract analysis uses machine learning and natural language processing to automatically review, extract, and analyze information from legal contracts. Modern AI systems can:

- **Identify key clauses** and obligations automatically
- **Flag potential risks** and unusual terms
- **Extract critical dates** and deadlines
- **Compare contracts** against standard templates
- **Predict outcomes** based on historical data
